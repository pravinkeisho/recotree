The Problem We Solve

High Compliance Cost

Repeated BIS testing, factory audits, and documentation increase cost and delay market access for MSMEs.

Lack of Trust

Testing agencies and regulators lack end-to-end traceability of samples, designs, and certifications.

Underutilized Testing Labs

Despite infrastructure, testing agencies see reduced volumes due to inefficient coordination.

The Recotree Solution

A permissioned blockchain platform connecting MSMEs, cluster associations, testing agencies, and regulators on a single source of truth.

Design Fingerprinting

Immutable hashing of product designs to enable sample reuse across factories.

Blockchain-based Testing Records

Secure, tamper-proof storage of test results and certificates.

Cluster-level Compliance

Shared compliance models reduce per-unit BIS certification costs.

QR-based Verification

Instant verification of compliance by buyers, regulators, and consumers.
